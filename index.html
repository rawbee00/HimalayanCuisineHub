Our Journey

Our story is a journey—one that begins high in the Himalayas, near the foot of Sagarmatha (Mount Everest), and winds its way to the tranquil shores of Costa del Silencio in Tenerife. Born from a deep love for the culinary traditions of Nepal and India, Himalayan Curry & Tandoor House brings you authentic flavors that have been passed down through generations.

From the bustling streets of Kathmandu to the spice markets of Delhi, we've gathered recipes that tell stories of mountain villages, royal kitchens, and family hearths. Each dish we serve carries the essence of the Himalayas—bold, warming, and deeply satisfying.
